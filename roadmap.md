# BlockchainHQ Earn: 6-Month BNB Chain Roadmap

**Platform:** Decentralized talent marketplace  
**Blockchain:** BNB Chain (Testnet → Mainnet)  
**Timeline:** January - June 2026

---

## Month 1-2: Foundation & Launch

- **BNB Mainnet Deployment** — Deploy audited smart contract to BNB Chain mainnet with verified source code on BSCScan
- **Enhanced Profiles** — Add portfolio showcase, social links verification, and skill tags with autocomplete
- **Real-time Notifications** — Email alerts, Discord webhook integration, and customizable notification preferences
- **Contract Explorer** — View live bounty status, on-chain transaction history, and contract balance directly in UI

---

## Month 3-4: Advanced Features

- **Milestone Payments** — Create bounties with multiple phases (e.g., 30% on design, 50% on development, 20% on completion)
- **Team Bounties** — Allow collaborative submissions with automatic prize splitting among 2-5 team members
- **Reputation System** — Star ratings, completion badges, on-chain reputation NFTs, and verified skill credentials
- **Advanced Search** — Filter by amount range, deadline, competition level, and save custom search preferences

---

## Month 5-6: Scale & Intelligence

- **AI Portfolio Builder** — Auto-generate professional resumes and portfolio websites from user profiles
- **Smart Recommendations** — "You're 80% qualified for this bounty. Learn X to qualify" with personalized skill paths
- **Dispute Resolution** — Community arbitration system with juror voting and staking mechanism
- **Multi-chain Support** — Deploy to opBNB for low-cost bounties and add cross-chain bridge functionality

## Key Features Summary

**Bounty Types**

- Single-winner & multi-winner bounties
- Milestone-based with phased payments
- Recurring bounties (weekly/monthly tasks)
- Team collaborations with auto prize-splitting

**User Experience**

- AI-powered candidate matching (GPT-4)
- Telegram notifications with match scores
- Portfolio builder & resume generator
- On-chain reputation & achievement NFTs

**Smart Contract**

- Trustless escrow with automatic payouts
- Dispute resolution via community voting
- Emergency pause & refund mechanisms
- Multi-chain support (BNB Chain + opBNB)
